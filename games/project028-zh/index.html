“The Escape of Worker”

On the subway to work? Waiting in line for a seat? When you are so bored, of course you should play “The Escape of Social Animals”!

【Anytime, anywhere, easy and relaxing】

The subway is so crowded that you can only move one hand? It doesn’t matter! Our operation is simple and easy to protect your liver!

You can play it while sitting, standing or lying down! Play and stop at any time!

【The plot is nonsensical and the imagination is penetrating】

“I am a social animal. You may not believe it. One day I worked overtime and slept in the company all night. When I woke up the next day, everyone turned into zombies!

I don’t know what to do. I can only continue to punch in and go to work every day. What if the boss returns to normal one day? I still need the full attendance award!

But it’s been a month, so I’d better go and find out if there are other people.

That’s it! Why do I have to go to work when the world is over!? Maybe, this is what social animals are like…”

【There are many weird and fun things, and the funny fun never stops】

Being weird must also be exquisite! Fresh and aerobic painting style! Delicate and smooth animation!

Surfing on the sea, exploring space, and fighting on the road! There are more than ten scenes like this! Going up to the sky and down to the ground, you can do anything! Escape around the world with the office worker!

“Don’t just introduce it! I’ve almost run half the world, why is there no one here!?”

Hmm… hold on, it’s almost there.
