  • Sword and Shadow





“Sword and Shadow” is a roguelike martial arts game.

Originally, there were no villains in the world. The world was peaceful. And I was just a swordsman who only knew one “basic sword move”.

Until one day, I found the sword.

I knew that as long as I pulled out the sword, all the magic skills in the world would be in my hands. And I also knew that the sword was the key to the formation that sealed the evil in the world.

But, so what? ! If the world was peaceful, what was the use of this swordsman? !

…

The evil in the world was intertwined into shadows, spreading all over the world. When you meet someone, as long as there is a seed of desire in your heart, it will take root and sprout, and then it will be out of control.

…

So there were villains in the world.

Oh, it also needs the biggest villain to save it.

[Game Features]

Ink silhouettes, martial arts charm.

Levels are randomly generated, and each game has a different experience!

Various schools have their own advantages and disadvantages; you can rely on your strength or your luck!

[Gameplay]

Roguelike-style martial arts level-breaking, one life to the end, happy revenge!

Dozens of moves and abilities can be freely combined to create your own martial arts school!

Real-time combat and turn-based system are combined, with both tension and strategy!
